Build Your Memory Map

How It Works

1 Enter the names of people, and they will be converted to train lines.

2 Enter memories you had with each person. Those memories become that person's train stations.

3 Memories with more than one person become transfer stations. Once finished, you can download your map as an image.

Read more about the methodology here. The map to the right is an example of a full map by Brian created using personal memories from 2004 to 2014.
